Who am I?

I am a passionate and versatile technology enthusiast based in Pune, with a strong drive to create impactful and efficient solutions across various domains. My expertise spans from crafting user-friendly web applications to developing robust software and engaging UI/UX designs. I thrive on challenges and continuously seek opportunities to apply my skills in Data Science, Application Development, Software Development, and Web Development to solve real-world problems.

My foundation in technology was built during my Masters in Computer Application at Sinhgad Institute Of Management, providing me with a comprehensive understanding of software development principles and practices. Since then, I have been actively expanding my skill-set and practical experience through diverse projects, collaborative endeavors, and a commitment to lifelong learning across the ever-evolving tech landscape.
